Privacy Note

Low-retention public build.

This public FundPilot surface is intentionally designed to avoid the highest-friction privacy patterns: no account wall, no third-party analytics, no ad-exchange scripts, and no personalized advertising.

What this public site does not do

No signup or login.

No third-party analytics pixels.

No Google AdSense, ad exchange tags, or personalized ad scripts.

No client-side local storage for user portfolios or watchlists.

What is still processed

Standard server access logs may exist at the hosting and reverse-proxy layer for security and operations.

Market and TEFAS data is fetched server-side from upstream providers and rendered into the page.

Affiliate or sponsor links may redirect you to third-party sites that have their own privacy practices.

Affiliate Disclosure

Direct placement only

How monetization works here

Placements are rendered directly by FundPilot as sponsor or affiliate cards. They are not injected by ad-tech vendors.

What changes the compliance posture

If you later add third-party analytics, cookie-based attribution, or Google ad products, consent and disclosure requirements increase materially.

Current practical posture

This build minimizes data collection, but no software or legal posture should be described as literally risk-free. Review hosting logs and future integrations before claiming that externally.
