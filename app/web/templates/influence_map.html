Why Transfer Entropy

Correlation is symmetric. Leadership is not.

Transfer entropy is useful because it measures directionality. That matters when deciding whether rates, volatility, crypto, or commodities are driving equities and gold, or merely moving with them.

This is a regime lens, not a trading trigger. Use it to frame risk leadership before you trust downstream setups.
